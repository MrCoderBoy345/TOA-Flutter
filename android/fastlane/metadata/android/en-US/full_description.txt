<b>The Orange Alliance</b> is a project to help make FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) information more accessible.

This app is the easiest way to access all the great content on http://theorangealliance.org on your Android device.

Are you a developer? Check out the source on GitHub: https://github.com/orange-alliance

Feel free to reach out to us through our Discord serve or contact us at contact@theorangealliance.org.
